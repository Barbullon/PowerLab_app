Welcome to Power Lab, Your New Climbing Companion!

Are you ready to take your training experience to the next level? Whether you're an elite climber or just starting your climbing journey, this app is here to assist you in reaching new heights !

What we do ?

Our app is designed to provide you with the most accurate and valuable data for your climbing workouts. By utilizing your smartphone, we can precisely track your movements, and give you insights into your performances. This data-driven approach will enable you to make informed decisions about your training, set realistic goals, and see tangible improvements in your climbing performance over time.

Key Features:

Until now, there was no simple and easily accessible way to measure power during climbing exercises. However, we know that power (among many other factors) is a key factor in climbing performance. This app proposes a simple protocol for measuring power during pull-up exercises. And as we say, you can't improve what you can't measure.

  • Data Aquisition: Get precise measurement of your movements during exercises using your smartphone.
  • Performance metrics: Get detailed information on your pull-up exercises, including power, peak power,force, velocity, displacement etc..
  • Score Interpretation: Get detailed explanation on the meaning of your results.
  • Test adaptability: Measure your pulling performances on any type of holds, with added weight or not.
  • Share Your Results: Export your results as graphics or Excel table and share them with whoever you want.
Why Does Power Training Matters in Climbing ?:

Climbing is a demanding sport in which you have to pull your weight upwards. To do this, your muscles contract to create a movement. Power is your ability to produce a quick movement under a large muscular load. In other words, it measures your ability to be both strong and fast. Power is essential for making explosive and long moves. And your ability to use your power wisely is an important skill for climbing efficiently. If you want to learn more about what is power, don't miss the "Score Info" button in this app.
