Why power matters in climbing:

	Climbing is a demanding sport that require to pull your weight up. 
	For this purpose, your muscle will contract to generate motion.
	Power is your ability to generate high force during a rapid motion. 
	In other words, it measures your capacity to be both strong and fast.
	This capacity is essential for making dynamic and explosive moves to overcome the crux of
	"Not dead but bien raide" located in La Roche aux Cros.
